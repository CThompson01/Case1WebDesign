Emerald Ash Borer

The emerald ash borer is an exotic beetle that was discovered in Michigan in 2002.

The larvae feed on the inner bark of ash trees, causing them to be unable to transport water.

Emerald ash borers have killed millions of ash trees since there discovery in North America.
